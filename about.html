Hello, I'm Gouri Kartha

I'm glad you're here! This space is where I pour pieces of myself into words—my thoughts, feelings, memories, and quiet observations of the world around me. Writing poetry has always been my way of making sense of life, capturing emotions I often can’t express out loud.

Some of these poems were born in moments of joy, some in silence and sadness, and others in between. They’re raw, real, and deeply personal. I don’t write to be perfect—I write to be honest. If my words resonate with even one soul, then this space has served its purpose.

I come from a background in English literature and media, and poetry has always been my first love. My inspirations come from everyday life—people I meet, places I go, nature, nostalgia, and moments that leave a mark on my heart.

I’ve had the joy of co-authoring two anthologies, available on Amazon. These experiences have only deepened my passion for writing and storytelling.

Thank you for reading, and for being part of this journey with me!
